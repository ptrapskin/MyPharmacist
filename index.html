MYPHARMACIST.CARE

Personal medication support for patients and caregivers.

MyPharmacist is built around a simple idea: medication help should feel personal. Instead of starting over with someone new each time, patients and families should be able to rely on one pharmacist who understands their medication story over time.

A pharmacist-led concept for more consistent medication guidance, coordination, and follow-through.

At mypharmacist.care, the vision is practical: help with questions, refill friction, medication changes, affordability issues, and the day-to-day complexity that often lands on patients or caregivers.

What MyPharmacist is meant to be

One reliable point of contact for medication questions, changes, and next steps.

  • Dedicated pharmacist relationship
    One pharmacist who learns your medications, your questions, and the context behind your care.
  • Medication change support
    Help when therapies are started, stopped, adjusted, delayed, or suddenly become confusing.
  • Cost and coverage navigation
    Support with Medicare drug plans, prior authorizations, affordability barriers, and savings options.
  • Caregiver-friendly communication
    A clearer line of support for adult children, spouses, and others helping manage medications for loved ones.

Why this matters

Medication problems grow when no one is clearly helping connect the dots.

MyPharmacist exists to explore a better model: one where medication support is longitudinal, easier to reach, and grounded in the real needs of patients and caregivers rather than brief, disconnected interactions.
